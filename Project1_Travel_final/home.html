Welcome to 'Oh the Places to Go"

Traveling is a passion that many people explore. However many people dont know where they want to go.
 
On this web site there are 5 countries and 1 state, for each location there is a large city chosen.
 
For each chosen city there are 2 FUN FACTS displayed, as well as a map (the map has markers at some hotels and restaurants, each marker is clickable, and each map is zoom and panning friendly)
 
We hope you will visit the place of your dreams.
